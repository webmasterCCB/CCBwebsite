## Help Keep the Music Playing

Classic City Band is a volunteer organization that relies on the generous support of individuals and businesses to continue bringing quality music to the Athens community. Your support helps us:

- Purchase music and pay licensing fees
- Rent rehearsal and performance spaces
- Maintain instruments and equipment
- Provide opportunities for our volunteer musicians
- Offer free or low-cost concerts to our community
